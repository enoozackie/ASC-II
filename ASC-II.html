  • Step 1: Each character is converted to its ASCII value
  • Step 2: All ASCII values are summed together
  • Step 3: The sum is divided by the table size
  • Step 4: The remainder (modulo) becomes the hash index
  • Why Modulo? It ensures the result is always within the table bounds (0 to tableSize-1)
  • Collisions: Different strings can produce the same hash index!
